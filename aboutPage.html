About us

Windstore is a store run by windsurfers for windsurfers. Windstore opened in 2010. Windstore is located at Richardson Beach in downtown, Kingston, Ontario. The Windstore owners are Karen and Gary. Gary is a CYA certified instructor and teaches individual and group lessons. Karen is the store manager and will provide you with the advice that you need to help you purchase the equipment that best suits your sailing ability and the conditions of your local sailing spot.

Windstore is open from 9am to 7pm daily from May 1 to November 30. The wind clause is in effect during the windsurfing season: Windstore is closed when it is 20knots! Outside the windsurfing season the store is open by appointment only (please email)
