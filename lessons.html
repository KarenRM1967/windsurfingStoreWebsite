Group Lessons

The first and third Sunday of every month there is group instruction at 1pm at Richardson Beach outside Windstore. If the wind cooperates this will be on the water. Otherwise there will be on land instruction using the simulator. Spots fill up quickly so please sign up at Windstore in advance. The cost is $20 per person and there are a maximum of 10 spots. If there is a particular skill you would like to improve, please talk to Karen or Gary at Windstore.

Individual Lessons

For those needing private instruction, Gary will teach one on one. The cost is $75 an hour. Please sign up at Windstore.
